Food - Reviews

Best £2.90 lunch in the canteen.

We rated every section of the canteen from hot to cold food. There was one clear winner.

There is a very serious discussion that happens every Monday morning in form time. It is not about school. It is about what you are going to buy in the canteen. We decided to settle it, once and for all. Three testers, one week, one canteen.

Here are the results, from worst to best.

5

School dinner

2 / 10 - go if desperate

The main meals are the usual contraversal pick. Sometimes maybe good, sometimes maybe bad. If you are hungry and thats the last option, we might accept it.

4

Sausage roll

6 / 10 - safe choice

Solid. Boring. You know exactly what you are getting. The queue is the main issue. If you can get there before the year nines you are stuck.

3

Sandwich Section

7 / 10 - underrated

People sleep on this one. It's not technically a meal deal but the maths works out. Warm bread is a whole experience.

2

Panini

8 / 10 - reliable winner

The classic. Acrosst the board favourite in many schools. An easy. reliable option.

1

Pasta Pot

10 / 10 - actually life-changing

An obvious fan favourite at Brockhill, many options to choose from to have with the pasta. Fills you up to keep you from hunger throughout the rest of the day.
