About

What is the BPPAC Magazine?

I make a lifestyle magazine aimed at people our age. Here is why.

TIDE started in year 10 because there weren't really any magazines that felt like they were for us students at Brockhill. There are loads of magazines for adults, and loads of stuff on TikTok, but nothing in the middle. Nothing that slows down for long enough to actually say something. So we started writing our own.

The name comes from the school that pupils attend - Brockhill Park Performing Arts College.

What do we write about?

We write about the things that actually take up space in our lives. Clothes, food, music, friendships, school, phones, bedrooms, Sundays. Normal things. We try to write about them properly instead of like a headline on your for you page.

We don't do trends, we don't do rankings of celebrities. Sorry.

Who reads it?

Mostly people at our school, honestly. But if you're in year 9, 10, 11 or 12 and this feels like your kind of thing then welcome. We're glad you're here.

Things people keep asking

How often do you publish?

Whenever I have the opportunity to go on the school laptops in Room 14. If you're lucky, maybe three issues a year?

Can I write for you?

Yes, probably. Come find us in Room 14 or send us a message. We are always looking for more people.

Is this actually a GCSE project?

Kind of, yeah. I don't know if i'll continue when im gone.
